Paginator Examples

Introduction

Here are some demos related to Paginator component.
You can find further information accesing to the Paginator official documentation.

Examples

Configuration

Config 1

Paginator with 5 total pages and 4 pages shown.

 <div class="c-paginator" data-params='{"nPages":5, "nPagesShown":4}'>

Config 2

Paginator activated by JS with 5 total pages and 4 pages shown.

HTML 
 <div class="c-paginator config-2">
Javascript 
$(".c-paginator.config-2").paginator({"nPages":10, "nPagesShown":4});

Config 3

Paginator with 6 total pages and 3 pages shown. "de" replaces "of" as counter delimiter.

 <div class="c-paginator" data-params='{"nPages":10, "nPagesShown":4, "settingsTheme": {"delimiter": " de "}}'>

Config 4

Paginator with 10 total and Zen mode activated.

 <div class="c-paginator" data-params='{"nPages":10, "nPagesShown":4, "settingsTheme": {"useZen": true}}'>
